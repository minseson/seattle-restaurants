Changes in Seattle restaurants in 2015

Seattle Restaurants is an attempt to document changes in the Seattle restaurant scene year to date in 2015. Data comes via the City of Seattle and is plotted on a map with shapefiles courtesy of Zillow using the D3 TopoJSON library. Restaurant classification is based on NAICS code.
